Calculator

This page provides you with a pay calculator based on wages and hours worked per week. You should enter a wage in GBP (selecting year/month/week/hour) and the weekly hours worked.

Once confirmed, the calculator provides the yearly, monthly, weekly and hourly wage.

Job Information

This page provides information about many different jobs.

Enter a general job title and then select one from a list of matching jobs. You'll then see a description, qualifications, tasks and most recent salary for that particular job.
